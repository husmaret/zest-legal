Frequently asked questions

How do I import a recipe?

Tap the + tab and choose one of three ways:

  • From a link — paste a URL and Zest extracts the title, ingredients, steps, and photo.
  • From a photo — photograph a cookbook page or handwritten card and Zest turns it into a structured recipe.
  • Manually — type the recipe in yourself.

Every import shows a review screen first, so you can fix anything before it lands in your library.

An import came out wrong — what can I do?

Automatic extraction isn't perfect, especially with unusual page layouts or hard-to-read handwriting. Edit the recipe after import (open it → Edit), or email us the source link/photo and we'll look into improving extraction.

How does finding friends from my contacts work?

Contact matching is optional and privacy-preserving: phone numbers are matched using one-way hashes, your address book is never stored on our servers, and nobody is notified that you searched. Details are in the privacy policy.

How do I make my account private?

Go to Profile → Settings and turn on Private account. New followers will then need your approval before they can see your friends-only recipes and cook posts.

How do I report content or block someone?

Open the recipe, cook post, or profile and tap the ⋯ menu → Report or Block user. Blocked users can be managed under Settings → Blocked users. We review reports promptly.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, recipes, cook posts, collections, follows, and all other data linked to you. See the privacy policy for retention details.

Which languages does Zest support?

Zest is fully available in English and German. You can change the language in Settings.
